AIN’T LIFE GRAND

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Danii Premium Vodka
  • 1 Lemon Twist

Preparation:

Stir cold for 60 seconds. Garnish with lemon twist

A LITTLE PARTY

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Danii Peach Vodka
  • 1 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • Peach Slice

Preparation:

Combine a in a shaker and shake cold. Pour over fresh ice and garnish with fresh peach slice and mint.

BEAUTIFUL LITTLE FOOL

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Danii Apple Vodka
  • 2 oz and pressed apple juice
  • Soda Water
  • Apple Slice
  • Pinch of Salt

Preparation:

Combine all except soda water in a shaker and shake cold. Strain into tall glass over fresh ice and top with soda. Garnish with a apple slice and pinch of salt.
