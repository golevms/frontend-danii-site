OUR STORY

Danii Vodka's story began several hundred years ago when Russian royalty created a vodka with unprecedented drinkability. For many years, these covetous aristocrats refused to share their vodka with anyone until the townspeople revolted and claimed Danii Vodka for everyone. They believed, and we believe to this day, that we can all share the Danii. Distilled today from that same secret formula,

Danii Vodka now pours luxury for everyone. While the recipe remains timeless and unchanged, the production process has been updated to reflect the latest technological advances. Today, Danii Vodka is the culmination of old-world opulence and modern-day innovation.
