DANII

Premium

Danii Premium is still made from the secret recipe taken from Russian noblemen in the 1800s. Created with our secret blend of limestone-filtered water and Moldovan wheat, Danii boasts a clean nose, a smooth palate, and a clean finish. Delicious, pure and free of additives

Danii Premium is an unadulterated luxury for all and compares favorably to some of the best vodkas in the world.

DANII

Apple

When Danii Premium meets the crisp flavor of apples, the mouthwatering result can be enjoyed on its own or in any number of cocktails. A crisp, apple-scented nose yields to a natural palate and a clean finish. A non-aggressive aftertaste, with afterglow reminiscent of an apple orchard, leaves you wanting for more.

DANII

Peach

Danii Peach combines the purity and drinkability of Danii Premium with the delightful yet subtle sweetness of peaches.

A delicately fruity nose yields to a flavorful, but not overpowering palate. Finally, a mellow yet sweet finish goes down smooth. All of the elements that make the original flavor successful blend seamlessly with the soft tartness of peaches, making Danii Peach an experience you won’t soon forget.
